Case Study: Nature Conservancy Canada (NCC)

Introduction: Canada is home to some of the world’s most breathtaking landscapes, from vast boreal forests to expansive prairies and coastal rainforests. Yet, these natural spaces are increasingly under threat from development, climate change, and resource extraction. As a response, Nature Conservancy Canada (NCC) has dedicated itself to safeguarding these precious ecosystems. By working across the country, NCC is not just conserving land but securing a future where biodiversity thrives. This case study explores the conservation challenges facing Canada and how NCC is tackling them.
